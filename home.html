How We Work

PixelStitch we are a team of friendly, approachable developers that will help you bring your website ideas to life. Between us we provide front-end (how it looks) and back-end (how it works) services and can help you create and maintain your online presence.

After discussing your ideas with the developers, we'll go away and make an in-depth plan of how we'll tackle the project. We'll get back to you with how long we think it will take and how much it will cost, including the small fee to host the website on the internet. If you want to go ahead with the project then we'll schedule in the work and get going! We adopt an Agile way of working, giving you two weekly updates on how your project is coming along. This gives you an opportunity to change anything you're not quite happy with, before the finished project is released.

When you're happy with the finished project, we will get your website live on the internet for you!
